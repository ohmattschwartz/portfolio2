About Me

After graduating from Skidmore College I headed West to Los Angeles where I spent seven oddly exciting/excitingly odd years (highlights include selling a pilot to the Jim Henson Company and starring in many national commercials you fast-forwarded through while watching Breaking Bad). Seeking a career rather than a series of jobs, I then moved to St. Petersburg, FL to learn how to talk computer at The Iron Yard.

It quickly became clear that this career-shift didn't mean I had to turn my back on the Creative Process. I love the act of taking a blank lightgrey page and a series of ideas and turning that into a fully-functioning website, game, or app. I look forward to continuing to direct that passion toward countless projects to come- maybe even yours.
